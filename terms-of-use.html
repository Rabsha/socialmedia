Pausing Your Monthly Plan

You have the option to pause your monthly plan at any time as long as you have a settled zero balance at the time of your pause request. Pauses can be made at any time with the exception of circumstances such as pausing due to a creative director notifying you of vacation time. You can either pause your plan for two weeks (for $295) or for an unlimited number of months (for $395 per month). You can also activate your account anytime during the pause and the activation date will be your new start date. Any outstanding days for your plan will be prorated to your reactivated plan. The followings are the benefit of pausing:

1. You can still consult with your designated creative director to get their brain for any sort of design and marketing matters. You'll keep your weekly video calls with your CD as usual and they will reply to your messages as quickly as before.

2. Your creative director will be always on standby to immediately work on projects whenever you're ready to activate your plan. They keep all your branding materials and source files ready so you don't need to start from scratch.

3. You get quarterly VIP consultation from our marketing experts to audit your brand, your website traffic, your marketing campaigns, and more to provide you with unique marketing ideas.
